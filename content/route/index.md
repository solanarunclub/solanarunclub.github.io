## Meeting point

Meet at the [drinking fountain](https://maps.app.goo.gl/xizjngNUfJ9XKtLK8) at 10:00:

![](./01-meeting-point.png)

At 10:10, set off towards the East. Pass [Speakers' Corner](https://maps.app.goo.gl/tRG91Eum9DL2iRzo8) and head towards Hyde Park Corner.

## Hyde Park Corner turn

When approaching Hyde Park Corner, you will notice the [Kiosk](https://maps.app.goo.gl/BFEBoaXW6bJBLSYr8) across the road:

![](./02-hyde-park-corner-kiosk.png)

Keep going clockwise, and take the rightmost path:

![](./03-hyde-park-corner-follow-clockwise.png)

![](./04-hyde-park-corner-rightmost-path.png)

## Follow along Rotten Row

Then just keep going straight along Rotten Row:

![](./05-rotten-row-followalong.png)

## West Carriage Drive crossing

Keep going until you reach the road crossing:

![](./06-south-road-crossing.png)

Cross the road but don't just keep going straight:

![](./07-south-road-crossing-cross.png)

Turn to the left after crossing the road, and keep going along the Flower Walk for a bit:

![](./08-south-road-crossing-turn-left.png)

## Albert Memorial turn

After a bit, you will reach a fork. You will see [Albert Memorial](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YKfryzHnBdout4hM8) on your left, and the path forward will be obstructed by a gate.

![](./09-flower-walk-fork.png)

Turn right here, and keep going straight:

![](./10-pre-lancaster-walk.png)

Soon, you will reach [Physical Energy Statue](https://maps.app.goo.gl/82a5G9p2ij5sromK7) blocking your path, but you'll need to keep going straight (and a little to the left) towards Lancaster Walk:

![](./11-lancaster-walk.png)

Continue towards Lancaster Gate, and take a right turn once you're about to reach it:

![](./12-lancaster-gate-turn.png)

Keep going straight along the Northern edge of the park. You will pass Italian Gardens:

![](./13-northern-edge.png)

## Another road crossing

Keep going until you reach another road crossing:

![](./14-north-road-crossing.png)

Cross the road, then follow the path as indicated on the image below:

![](./15-path-after-crossing.png)

![](./16-path-after-crossing-2.png)

## Final stretch

Keep going straight until you reach the water fountain again:

![](./17-final-stretch.png)

![](./18-water-fountain.png)
